Description

You are locked in an epic D&D battle with Death.
A combat encounter takes place in series of actions, until one side is dead or withdraws. As this is fight to the death, however, retreat is not an option. Combatants have hit points (health). Once a combatant reaches -10 HP, he dies.
There are two types of actions that a player can take during his turn: take cover, lose cover, or attack. Sometimes multiple actions can be performed in a row.
Attack
An attack can be made with
  1. A two-handed weapon
  2. A one-handed weapon
  3. Two one-handed weapons
  4. A missle weapon (bow and arrow, javalin, ninja star, etc.)
For an attack, the attacker roles a d20 (20-sided die).
  1. If he rolls a 1, the attack fails.
  2. If he rolls a 20, the attack succeeds.
  3. Otherwise, take the result and add his Attack and Strength modifier. If he uses a missle weapon, use the Dexterity modifier instead of Stength. If that total exceeds the opponent's Armor class, the attack is successful. For a two-weapon attack, do this seperately for each weapon, but add -6 for the first one, and -10 for the second.
Damage
For each weapon that successfully hit the opponent, do the following: The base damage is determined by the weapon. If it was a non-missle weapon, add the attacker's Strength modifier. If the weapon is two-handed, increase the Strength modifier contribution by 50% (rounding up). The opponent's health is reduced by the amount of damage.
Other
Cover provides +4 to a combatant's armor. The bonus begins once a compatant gains cover, and lasts until he loses it.
To make things fair, both sides will have one two-handed weapon, two one-handed weapons, and a missle weapon.
Given all the details game between you and Death, determine the winner.

Input

The first line is five integers between 0 and 100 inclusive: your character's hit points, strength, dexterity, attack, and armor.
The second line is four positive integers, A, B, C, D, between 0 and 100 inclusive, for the damage of the your two-handed, the two one-handed, and the missle weapons (in that order).
The next two lines are the same, but for Death's character.
The fifth line is 
N
, the number of actions in the game. 
1 ≤ N ≤ 2000

The next 
N
lines are the actions. Each action has multiple space-separated parameters. The first is always the comabatent, I (you), or D (death). The second is the action itself.
  1. + is taking cover.
  2. - is losing cover.
  3. A, B, C, D is an attack, corresponding to the weapon used. If there are two weapons used, they are given without space, in the order of use (e.g. CB).
In the case of an attack, there is a third parameter, the result of the d20 roll.

Output

Output SURVIVE, if you win the battle, and DIE if Death wins.
